KEPS Parents Discord

A simple, private space for parents to ask questions, share resources, and stay informed about what is happening at school.

Join the Discord community

Discord is like a group chat plus notice board. You can use it in your browser or install the free app on your phone or computer. When you join, you will see a very short screen with a couple questions. This is just a quick check to confirm you are part of the KEPS parent community.

New to Discord? That is normal. Most parents are. If you do not have a Discord account yet, you will be asked to create and verify one (usually by confirming your email or phone) before you can join the server. You can always ask other KEPS parents for help if you get stuck.

Frequently asked questions

Do I have to be ‚Äútech savvy‚Äù?

No. If you can use email or text messaging, you can use Discord.

Is Discord free?

Yes. You do not have to pay for anything to join or use this server.

What about notifications?

By default, our server is set so that you only get notified when you are mentioned. You can mute channels or adjust alerts at any time. There are simple instructions in #getting-started.

I do not want to use Discord. Can I still stay informed?

Yes. You can still hear about important things through school channels and other parents. This Discord is just an extra, optional way to stay connected.
